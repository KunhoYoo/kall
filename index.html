Refunds and cautions

Please check before requesting.

Refund policy

  • After your request is received, contact support within 10 minutes if you want to cancel.
  • After 10 minutes, call preparation and staff assignment begin, so payment is processed normally.
  • After that window, refunds are not available regardless of the reservation result.

Requests that need extra review

  • Restaurants with deposits or cancellation fees are not handled through the standard flow.
  • Omakase, Michelin-listed, and similar deposit-based restaurants require 1:1 support first.
  • If Kall pays the deposit on your behalf, a 10% handling fee is added to the deposit amount.
Example: a ₩200,000 deposit becomes ₩220,000 including the fee

No-show notice

  • If you cannot visit on the day of the reservation or the day before, contact Kall as soon as possible.
  • A no-show directly harms the business that held the seat or time for you.
  • If you need cancellation or changes, tell us early so we can call the business in time.
